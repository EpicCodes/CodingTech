What is Scratch?

Scratch is a coding language and online community where you can create your own interactive stories, games, and animations, and art ,and share your creations with others around the world. As young people create and share Scratch projects, they learn to think creatively, reason systematically, and work collaboratively. Scratch is a project of the Scratch Foundation in collaboration with the Lifelong Kindergarden group at the MIT Media Lab. It is available for free at https://scratch.mit.edu.
